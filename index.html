ABOUT ME

My name is Siddharth Gautam, born in the year 2002. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at Galgotia College of Engineering and Technology, located in Greater Noida. My academic endeavors are driven by a passion for technology and its boundless potential to shape the world.
In addition to my academic pursuits, I have had the privilege of serving as the former Management Head of the Rotaract Club, showcasing my commitment to leadership and community engagement. This experience has instilled in me a sense of responsibility and a deep understanding of the value of teamwork and effective communication.
My skill set encompasses a diverse range of capabilities, including Web development, web design, graphic design, UI design utilizing Figma, and front-end development. Through dedicated learning and hands-on experience, I have honed these skills to a professional standard, aiming to contribute to the digital landscape with creativity and precision.
I am enthusiastic about leveraging my knowledge and skills to make a meaningful impact in the realm of technology. My aspiration is to continue evolving, both personally and professionally, in order to deliver innovative solutions that enhance user experiences and contribute positively to society.
